Our Philosophy

Substance Over Hype.
But Never at the Expense of Style.

At Substance Salon & Barberspa, we believe a great cut isn't a product — it's a practice. We've built a space where salon artistry and barber mastery share the same floor, the same standards, and the same obsession with quality.

Located on Lexington Ave in Clifton, NJ, we've grown entirely through word of mouth and earned trust. Our team brings decades of combined experience across color science, precision cutting, hot-towel spa services, and texture treatments — all under one roof designed for everyone.
